Historic Sites

From tall city high rises, to entire islands, San Francisco holds many historical sites. Some of these places have been standing for several decades, and some even centuries. Looking at these places, we can learn a lot about the events that the historic sites have seen. These places can even tell us the story of the people that were involved in making those places the way they are now.

Landmarks

Alcatraz

An island that is off the mainland of San Francisco, Alcatraz is best known for holding some of the most dangerous prisoners there where within its facility.

Coit Tower

This tower holds thousands of square feet of murals, as well as one of the best views that you can get of the city.
